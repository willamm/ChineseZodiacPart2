The Monkey

The Monkey (猴) is the ninth of the 12-year cycle of animals which appear in the Chinese zodiac related to the Chinese calendar. The Year of the Monkey is associated with the Earthly Branch symbol 申. People born in the Year of the Monkey tend to be optimistic, confident, and curious. They can also be egotistical, vain, and snobbish.

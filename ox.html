The Ox

The Ox (丑) is the 2nd of the 12-year cycle of animals which appear in the Chinese zodiac related to the Chinese calendar. The Year of the Ox is denoted by the Earthly Branch symbol 丑. People born in the Year of the Ox are said to not get along with people born in the Year of the Rat. This is because the order of the animals in the zodiac was determined in a race. In this race, the Rat cheated by jumping off of the Ox in order to win.

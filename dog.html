The Dog

The Dog (狗) is one of the 12-year cycle of animals which appear in the Chinese zodiac related to the Chinese calendar. The Year of the Dog is associated with the Earthly Branch symbol 戌. The character 狗 refers to the actual animal while 戌 refers to the zodiac animal. People born in the Year of the Dog tend to be loyal, honest, and kind. They can also be pessimistic, critical, and poor communicators.
